About RecipeApp

RecipeApp is a modern web application designed to help users manage, search, and explore delicious recipes with ease. Add your favorite recipes, update them anytime, and browse through a curated list of meals. Our platform focuses on simplicity, usability, and performance.

Built with Django and Bootstrap, RecipeApp ensures a responsive, dark-themed experience that is easy on the eyes while providing all the functionalities a modern recipe management app needs.

Features

  • Add new recipes with images and descriptions
  • Update existing recipes with ease
  • Search recipes quickly using the built-in search
  • Dark mode interface for comfortable viewing
  • Responsive design for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices
